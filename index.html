How Scholify helps

Scholify explains the difference between Aadhaar-linked and DBT-enabled accounts, shows simple checks, and connects you to help.

Aadhaar-linked

Aadhaar is attached to the account — but not always DBT-enabled for government transfers.

DBT-enabled (Aadhaar-seeded)

Properly seeded account that can receive Direct Benefit Transfers (scholarship money).
